About Hameeda Hossain

Upload portrait
photo here

Hameeda Hossain is one of Bangladesh's foremost human rights defenders, a legal scholar, writer, and founding figure of the women's movement in the country. Her work spans law, journalism, activism, and institution-building across more than five decades.

She co-founded Ain o Salish Kendra (ASK), Bangladesh's leading legal aid and human rights organisation, and served as its executive director for many years. She has been a consistent voice for democratic rights, minority protections, and gender justice in both national and international forums.

As a writer and editor, she shaped public discourse through the magazines and publications she built, her extensive journalism, and her essays on law, politics, and society. This collective gathers the breadth of that work in one place.

This archive is maintained by her collective. To contribute materials or corrections, please reach out via the repository.
